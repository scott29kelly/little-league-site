# Research Notes

## Platform Context

- Middletown-Neshaminy Baseball currently uses Sports Connect for public registration.
- Sports Connect should remain the system of record for registration, waivers, family accounts, and official league records.
- The public site should keep the registration destination easy to update.

## Navigation Pattern

Live youth-sports sites commonly use direct parent-facing labels such as Registration, Schedules, Field Locations, About, Sponsors, Volunteer, Gallery, FAQ, and Contact. For this demo, the clearest structure is:

- Registration
- Schedule & Fields
- About
- Gallery
- Sponsors
- FAQ

## Current Site Audit Findings

Public pages reviewed on the existing Blue Sombrero/Sports Connect site:

- Home

- Travel
- Wild Baseball
- Thunder Softball
- Sponsors
- Check out our sponsors
- Public field directory view

Useful public details pulled into the demo:

- Direct Stack Sports/Sports Connect sign-in URL from the visible Register button.
- Baseball serves players ages 4-16.
- In-house divisions are T-Ball, Coach Pitch, Rookies, Minors, Majors, Juniors, and Seniors.
- Travel baseball tryout details are announced through Facebook and league email.
- Baseball practices are commonly Wednesday, with games commonly Friday and/or Sunday during the season.
- Public field directory includes exact addresses and subfield names for MAA @ Twin Oaks Park, Walter Miller Elementary, Carl Sandburg Fields, Penndel, Schweitzer, Everitt, Pearl Buck, and Dunkin Field.
- Public Facebook page identifies MAA as a local nonprofit youth sports organization with the main address at 2897 Trenton Road, Levittown, PA.
- Public Facebook About page lists the MAA registration email and service area.
- Public board page lists baseball board and operations contacts.
- Public sponsor pages list community sponsors and sponsor-recognition options.
- Current sponsor sources identify Platinum, MVP, and community sponsors. The demo uses locally saved sponsor logos from the public sponsor pages and links sponsors to public business or representative sites when available.
- Sponsorship recognition options include field banners, team recognition, website/social recognition, and event or game-day support.

Details intentionally not copied:

- Sponsor prices, sponsorship package rates, refund amounts, family fees, and other dollar amounts.
- Actual Facebook child/player photos, because final public gallery images should be rights-approved by the league.
- Real-time field status behavior, because the replacement site should use a simple admin-updated game-day notice instead.

## Product Implication

The demo should avoid building custom registration or a real-time field-availability system. The safer path is a polished public website that points families into Sports Connect, shows one admin-updated game-day notice, and provides field-location details families can reference.
